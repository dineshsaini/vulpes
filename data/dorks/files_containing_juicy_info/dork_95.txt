Description:      intitle:"Usage Statistics for" "Generated by Webalizer"


The webalizer program shows web statistics for web servers. This information includes who is visiting the site, what pages they visit, error codes produced, filetypes hosted on the server, number of hits, referrers, exit pages, and more which can provide very interesting recon information for an attacker.
